Lime3DS is an open-source, easy-to-use Nintendo 3DS emulator

Forked from Citra, Lime3DS has all of the features you have come to expect from a 3DS emulator.

Our mission is to continue to further the progress of 3DS emulation, implementing new features and making constant improvements for the benefit of your gaming experience.

As a community effort, this project is only possible due to the work of passionate enthusiasts. Together, the future of 3DS emulation can be bright.
