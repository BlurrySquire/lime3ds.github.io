FAQ

Why does nothing happen when I open Lime3DS?

There 3 executables that come with Lime3DS named 'lime3ds-cli', 'lime3ds-room', and 'lime3ds-gui'.

Please ensure you are opening the program named 'lime3ds-gui'.


Why does Lime3DS crash repeatedly?

Please ensure that your device reaches the minimum specifications required to use Lime3DS.

You can find these in our GitHub readme.


How does Lime3DS compare to Citra Enhanced?

While Lime3DS and Citra Enhanced were both created from Citra's demise, we have expressedly different goals.

Lime3DS aims to continue Citra's development as it always was and maintain the status quo with a focus on stability, careful consideration and proper procedure with all changes made to the emulator.

Citra Enhanced on the other hand focuses on advanced "hacks" which attempt to squeeze as much performance out of games as possible at the cost of stability and code sanity.

Both forks have their own independent purpose for existing, and are not competing projects.
